## What Trenova does

Trenova replaces fragmented spreadsheets and costly legacy TMS platforms with a single, cohesive system built for modern Over-The-Road carriers.

  <Card title="Shipment Management" icon="truck" href="/features/shipments">

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy Trenova">
    Pull the Docker images and run the setup script on your server. See the [Deployment Overview](/deployment/overview) for prerequisites and step-by-step instructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your organization">
    Log in with the default admin credentials, change your password, and set up your organization settings. See [Organizations](/admin/organizations).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your fleet and workers">
    Create equipment types, add your tractors and trailers, then onboard your drivers. See [Fleet Management](/features/fleet) and [Worker Management](/features/workers).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your first shipment">
    Set up a customer, configure service types, and dispatch your first shipment. See the [Quick Start](/quickstart) guide for a complete walkthrough.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Trenova is currently in active development and not yet recommended for production use. APIs and features may change before the stable release.
</Note>
